📋 PILOT BRIEFING

Controls

← → Arrow Keys to move | Spacebar to fire | P or Esc to pause

Enemies

🪨 Asteroid — 1 hit, 10 pts. Falls straight down. Costs a life at the bottom.
☄️ Big Asteroid (Comet) — 2 hits, 20 pts. Glowing core with flame tail. Spawns from Level 2.
💀 Boss Asteroid — 3 hits, 50 pts. Every 6th kill after 15s. Grants +2 lives!

Lives

Start with 3 ❤️ lives (max 5). Asteroids reaching the bottom cost a life.

Power-ups

🔵 Shield — 6s invincibility with blue glow
🟠 Rapid Fire — Faster shooting for 5–8s
🟢 Spread Shot — 3 bullets in a fan
💗 Extra Life — +1 life (rare), max 5
🔷 Time Slow — Enemies at half speed
⭐ Score Multiplier — Double points for ~8–10s
💣 Screen Clear — Destroys all on-screen enemies instantly

Leveling

Every 150 points advances a level. Enemy speed and spawn rate scale up. Game freezes briefly on level-up.

Touch Controls

◀ Left button — hold to move ship left
▶ Right button — hold to move ship right
🔥 Fire button — tap or hold to shoot (250ms cooldown)
⏸ Pause button — tap the Pause button at the top to pause

Enemies

🪨 Asteroid — 1 hit, 10 pts. Costs a life at the bottom.
☄️ Big Asteroid (Comet) — 2 hits, 20 pts. Spawns from Level 2.
💀 Boss Asteroid — 3 hits, 50 pts. Every 6th kill after 15s. Grants +2 lives!

Lives

Start with 3 ❤️ lives (max 5). Asteroids reaching the bottom cost a life.

Power-ups

🔵 Shield · 🟠 Rapid Fire · 🟢 Spread Shot · 💗 Extra Life
🔷 Time Slow · ⭐ Score Multiplier · 💣 Screen Clear
Fly into the power-up icon to collect it!
